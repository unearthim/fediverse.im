Social media was built on walled gardens. The future is an open, federated universe.

The Fediverse is a network of thousands of independent social communities that can all talk to each other. It is a web of protocols, not platforms, where users have the freedom to choose their home, control their data, and own their identity.

This is the landmark for the heart of that movement. For the definitive guide to the federated universe, the foundational protocol that connects its worlds, or the platform that makes joining it simple and safe for everyone.
